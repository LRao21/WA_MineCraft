The “Exploring Exterior Design Elements” group involves snippets from experiences designing the exteriors of builds. Particularly, they focus on specific design elements, such as window and roof design, instead of the larger scale of builds. Details lie in thought, and that is something we wish to explore through crossroads.

To elaborate, the current snippets are drawing of the roof from the “Wooden House” from the Minecraft Handbooks, the window design from the mountainous palace built by FyreUK, and an outline of one of our team member's favorite village structures:the Desert Biome Blacksmith House. Combined, these images represent design choices others—players and Mojang alike—made in world planning, highlighting the significance of small decisions in overall builds.
