Profiles: What's in Jeffrey's Village?

Image Credits: Minecraft Wiki

Structure: Animal Pen

Biome: Plains
Description: 5 by 6 block area of grass enclosed by oak wood fence.
Purpose: House/Capture docile mobs such as pigs, sheeps, and cows.
Jeffrey's Taste: 3/10, ;
The space is taken up without frequent use. Shawn also does not like it. As a villager, I am indifferent, but as their friend I am not.

Structure: Large House

Biome: Plains
Description: 5 by 9 block, 2-story home made with oak wood, cobblestone, and glass.
Purpose: (Unspecialized) Housing for villagers.
Jeffrey's Taste: 7/10 ;
The space at a glance is similar to mine. The two-story structure provides ample space, in my opinion. The structure overall is not an eyesore. The villagers living there are also pleasant. It could, however, have benefited from a balcony.

Structure: Temple

Biome: Plains
Description: 3-story home made with a bordered open roof using cobblestone, and glass.
Purpose: (Unspecialized) Place of Worship for villagers.
Jeffrey's Taste: 9/10 ;
The space at a glance is similar to mine. The three-story structure provides ample space for one to find peace with themselves in silence, in my opinion. Although it could benefit from more decoration, its simplistic style and purpose as a place of congregration snd community outweights its asthetic simplicity. Personally, I have never had an issue. from a balcony.

Structure: Pathways

Biome: Plains
Description: Rough pathways throughout the village made with padded dirt.
Purpose: Navigation throughout the village.
Jeffrey's Taste: 2/10 ;
The pathways came to be because we all walked there. There is no sense of unity to them. I hear other places have actual roads. The village would not be at a loss if they were to humor implementing those hearings here.

Structure: Library [Jeffrey's Home!]

Biome: Plains
Description: Jeffrey's Home; A 2-story library with books focusing on design, arts, and writing.
Purpose: (Specialized) Housing for Jeffrey, Storage for Village Knowledge
Jeffrey's Taste: 10/10 ;
It is my home, after all.

Structure: Armorer's Home

Biome: Plains
Description: 6 by 7 block, single story building made with brick, oak wood, and cobblestone.
Purpose: (Specialized) Provide furnished armor and tools to those willing to trade for them.
Jeffrey's Taste: 7/10 ;
The fellow who works there is rather pleasant. Although the place is rather noisy, it seems of sound foundation. Their tools are useful as well.
