What is MineCraft CrossRoads?

What We Know About Steve

Based on the Wandering Trader that visits the village every couple of weeks, Steve appears to be one of his clients. After many lengthy discussions over the worth of bamboo sticks, the Trader mentioned this strange being. We consider them strange for the Trader made it clear that many of their features resembled ours yet did not at the same time.

Who Runs MineCraft Crossroads?

MineCraft Crossroads is run by a team of three members of our world (the "server" as Steve calls it.) Our members met at our founder's village through inivitations from them, joining the team part-time and contributing to its development through their unique experiences with architecture in the different parts of the world, also keeping a lookout for the infamous "Steve."
